See all perspectives, find the best solution, liberate the mind from the mundane.

These three phrases encapsulate my approach to software engineering. They reflect my desire to help free the client's time and energy to focus on meaningful questions. When an engineer's solution is good, the client transcends the tool, operating on a level beyond what was previously possible. When the developer's output has satisfied the client and the client can produce at a higher level of achievement is my idea of success.

Skills

Full stack development is my professional expertise, including database design, backend and frontend programming, rapid prototyping, and enterprise-level collaboration. My solutions have earned me accolades at Hackathons, and saved enterprise-level operations millions of dollars. It would seem that technology's current abilty to empower people and organizations is unprecented.

Equally fluent are my presentation skills, developed from 100+ recitals given as a pianist and hundreds of lessons given as an educator of young adults. As such, my additional expertises include education/instruction, and classical music performance.

Recent interests include developing tools for educators, and reading the great works of fiction in both the English and Simpified Chinese scripts. Another hobby of great interest is to cook and enjoy food of all varieties.
